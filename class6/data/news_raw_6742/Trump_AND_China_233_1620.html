Clinton and Trump Do Battle in the Final Presidential Debate


BYLINE: NICHOLAS KRISTOF


SECTION: OPINION


LENGTH: 1343 words



HIGHLIGHT: Nicholas Kristof live-tweets and fact checks the showdown.


Hillary Clinton and Donald Trump faced off tonight in their final presidential debate. To me the big headline was Trump's refusal to commit to honoring the result. That is unprecedented, more in keeping with a warlord than with a major party nominee, and it leaves me saddened. Here's my play-by-play, a first draft of history, in the form of selected tweets.
