Our article on July 10 by Stephanie Rosenbloom about staying at Trump Hotels (''Checking In at Trump Hotels'') drew many responses. Below are two, edited. 

Assessing the Trump Brand

Who is going to pay for the security at Trump-branded hotels and golf courses if Donald J. Trump were to become president? All of these places would be obvious terrorist targets.

WINSTON SMITH

Too bad you failed to mention that so many of the accouterments at Trump hotels are made outside the U.S. -- bathrobes made in China, bath towels made in Egypt, etc. If Mr. Trump's going to ''Make America Great Again,'' maybe he ought start by having all the paraphernalia bearing his name ''Made in America!''

STEPHAN CUTLER
