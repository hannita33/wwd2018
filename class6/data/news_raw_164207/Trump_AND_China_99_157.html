DT names China critic top trade aide


BYLINE: Daniel Halper


SECTION: All Editions; Pg. 10


LENGTH: 69 words


Donald Trump has selected China hawk Peter Navarro to lead the White House National Trade Council, the president-elect's transition team announced Wednesday.

Navarro, an economics professor at the University of California, Irvine, is the author of "Death by China: How America Lost its Manufacturing Base."

"I read one of Peter's books on America's trade problems years ago and was impressed," Trump said.
