Hillary's 'Foreign Policy': Is It Just Trump-Bashing?


BYLINE:


SECTION: All Editions; Pg. 22


LENGTH: 333 words


The Issue: Hillary Clinton's foreign-policy speech, in which she portrayed Donald Trump as "dangerous."

It was important to hear Hillary Clinton speculate about the potential failures of Donald Trump's foreign policy ("Hillary Describes Hillary," Editorial, June 3).  

She ought to know. After all, she has a long track record of actual failures. Rich Codey

Manhattan

The Post can spin what Hillary said about Trump all it wants, but the truth is he's an imperious, impetuous man-boy who shouldn't have knowledge of the nuclear codes.

Gary Schwartz

Fort Lee, NJ

Clinton gave a 43-minute speech on her foreign policy.

But she spent 33 minutes of it droning on and on about Trump. That's her foreign policy?

No wonder President Obama and Clinton messed things up around the world over the last seven years. They don't even know what foreign policy is.

That's how she plans to defeat Islamic terror, by attacking Trump?

That's what she'll do with the Iran nuke problem? Tell us we should be afraid of Trump? That's how she'll deal with ISIS, Russia and China? John Scott

Dumont, NJ

Hillary has a long track record; Trump should attack it.

She seems calculated, but she's really not.

All she has on him are hypotheticals and what he might have said at some point. She's baiting him to say something that will cost him dearly.

Trump should take the high road. It'll pay off in November.

Nick Palazzo

Stamford, Conn.

Hillary just doesn't get it.

Her e-mails aren't an issue for me. I don't care about them.

What I do care about, however, is how she conducted herself as secretary of state. She didn't act in the best interests of the United States or its partners.

She says "Look at my vast experience," but what has she accomplished?

Nothing positive came from any of her decisions. After all the lies over the Iranian deal, how can the people trust her?

We'll wind up with nothing better than President Obama 2.0.

Alan Brooks

Brooklyn
