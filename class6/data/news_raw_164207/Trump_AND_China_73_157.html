President-Elect Trump China diplo's DT red alert


BYLINE: Daniel Halper


SECTION: Metro; Pg. 6


LENGTH: 82 words


China has launched a shot across Donald Trump's bow, with its US ambassador warning him its national sovereignty and territorial integrity are not "bargaining chips."

"The political foundation of China-US relations should not be undermined. It should be preserved," Cui Tiankai said Wednesday at a DC meeting with business leaders.

The remarks follow Trump's comments on the "One China" policy and his decision to take a call on Dec. 2 from the democratically elected leader of Taiwan.
